---
title: "Starter Toolkit: The 6 Docs Every Young Leader Needs"
summary: Lightweight pages you can duplicate and use immediately.
tags: [toolkit, templates]
---

1) **One‑Page Plan** — goals, 3 priorities, owners, date.  
2) **Decision Log** — date, choice, why, owner, result.  
3) **Run‑of‑Show** — minute‑by‑minute for events/meetings.  
4) **Roles Sheet** — who owns what this week.  
5) **Feedback Bank** — things to keep, start, stop.  
6) **Retrospective** — what went well, what to change next time.

**Try this today:** Make the One‑Page Plan and share the link.
