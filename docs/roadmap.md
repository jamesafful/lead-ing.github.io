title: Roadmap to Leadership (0 → 2)
description: A simple progression from self‑management to leading small teams.
---

**Stage 0 → 1: Self leadership**
- Show up prepared. Track commitments. Communicate early.

**Stage 1 → 1.5: Peer leadership**
- Run crisp meetings. Share context. Remove blockers for others.

**Stage 1.5 → 2: Team leadership**
- Own outcomes. Plan, delegate, follow through, reflect.
